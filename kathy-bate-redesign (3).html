About Kathy

Interiors in celebration of people gathering over food.

With a portfolio spanning over 40 years, Kathy Bate is one of the Bay Area's most successful and seasoned design specialists. She is extremely knowledgeable about cabinetry, appliances, plumbing fixtures, furnishings, lighting, and finishes.

She uses eco-friendly and sustainable products and one-of-a-kind craftsmen options that make your kitchen or restaurant come to life as uniquely yours. Contractors, vendors, and architects appreciate Kathy's detailed CAD drawings and personal involvement, and gladly refer clients to her — and call on her to design their own homes.

Kathy has also shared her love of design as an instructor at Chabot College in Hayward and Los Positas College in Livermore, CA.
