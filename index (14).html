Our Story

Over 60 years of
excellence in the
East Bay.

Contra Costa Ballet Centre has been the East Bay's premier destination for serious classical ballet training since its founding. Located in the heart of Walnut Creek, our school has produced professional dancers who have gone on to perform with American Ballet Theatre, San Francisco Ballet, New York City Ballet, Pacific Northwest Ballet, and companies around the world.

We believe that the discipline, artistry, and dedication required by ballet are gifts that extend far beyond the stage. Every student — from a five-year-old in their first ballet slippers to an adult discovering movement for the first time — is met with the same standard of care and excellence.

Common Questions

Everything you need
to know before
you begin.

Still have questions? We are always happy to speak with you directly.

Contact Us
What age can my child start ballet at CCB?
+

Our Children's Division welcomes students starting at age 5. For our youngest dancers, we also offer a Summer Camp program for ages 4–7, which is a wonderful first introduction to music and movement before entering the formal Children's Division.

Do you offer a trial class before enrolling?
+

Yes — we warmly encourage prospective families to contact us about attending a trial class. This gives your child the chance to experience our studio, meet their teacher, and make sure the fit feels right before any commitment is made.

How does the Student Division program work?
+

The Student Division serves dancers ages 7–18 with a progressive curriculum that builds in intensity and artistry year over year. All students participate in our annual performances at professional venues including the Lesher Center and Kaiser Center in Oakland.

What is the Summer Intensive program?
+

The 2026 Summer Intensive runs June 22 through July 17 and is designed for serious students ages 7–18. Training is intensive and daily, covering classical technique, repertoire, and performance preparation with faculty of the highest professional calibre.

I'm an adult with no experience. Can I join Open Division?
+

Absolutely. Our Open Division was designed precisely for adults and teens of all backgrounds — from complete beginners to returning dancers. Drop-in classes are available, and our faculty welcome mixed-level classes with clarity, patience, and warmth.

Where is CCB located?
+

We are located at 2040 N. Broadway, Walnut Creek, CA 94596 — in the heart of the East Bay. Call us at (925) 935-7984 for directions or parking information.
